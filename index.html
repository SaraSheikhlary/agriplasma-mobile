Mission Statement

We capture the fourth state of matter — cold atmospheric plasma, nature’s lightning chemistry made precise and safe — and turn it into an intelligent, chemical-free force for soil disinfection, nitrogen fixation, and microbiome activation. Through portable and autonomous robotic systems, we give farmers, researchers, and space pioneers the power to heal soil on demand, anywhere.

Vision Statement

To ignite a plasma-powered renaissance in agriculture — restoring the living chemistry of Earth’s soils today and laying the foundation for sustainable food systems across the solar system tomorrow.

The Vision

Modern agriculture is running on borrowed time. Synthetic chemicals poison the microbiome, deplete fertility, and leave toxic legacies in our food and water. Meanwhile, humanity dreams of becoming multi-planetary — yet every conventional fertilizer or pesticide we send to Mars becomes a logistical and ecological liability.

AgriPlasma Mobile™ was born at the intersection of plasma physics, precision agritech, robotics, and space biotechnology. It delivers nature’s most elegant soil-repair mechanism — reactive oxygen and nitrogen species (RONS) generated by cold atmospheric plasma — in a form factor that fits in a farmer’s hand or roams fields autonomously.

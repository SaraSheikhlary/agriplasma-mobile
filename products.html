Our Product

AgriPlasma Mobile™

The AgriPlasma Mobile™ is a lightweight, portable plasma-powered device designed to mimic the ease of a vacuum cleaner but powered by advanced dielectric barrier discharge (DBD) technology.

  • On-Site Neutralization: Deactivates pathogens and breaks down pesticide residues instantly.
  • Nitrogen Fixation: Boosts soil nutrient profiles without chemical fertilizers.
  • Plug-and-Play: Ergonomic design with simple manual, Bluetooth, or Wi-Fi controls for precision emission.

Autonomous AgriPlasma Mobile

A compact, cube-shaped smart robot that takes everything the Mobile version does and makes it fully autonomous.

Equipped with wheels, onboard sensors, ozone monitoring, a touchscreen interface, and seamless connectivity to the AI tools, the Bot navigates fields on its own, optimizes plasma intensity and coverage in real time, and operates 24/7 with minimal human oversight. Deploy a fleet and watch your soil heal itself while you sleep.

This system is designed from day one for edge AI intelligence, real-time diagnostics, treatment logging, predictive optimization based on soil data, weather, and crop needs, and future integration with digital-twin soil models.

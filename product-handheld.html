Handheld AgriPlasma Mobile™

Shaped like a high-tech vacuum cleaner but powered by cutting-edge dielectric barrier discharge (DBD) plasma, this lightweight, wheeled unit lets you walk your fields, gardens, or greenhouses and treat soil on the spot.

Draw in ambient air (or optional N₂/O₂/Ar mixtures), accelerate it through a Venturi, ionize it between precision electrodes, and direct a stream of cold plasma through a flexible, heat-resistant hose directly onto the soil. Pathogens die. Pesticide residues break down into harmless compounds. Atmospheric nitrogen is fixed into plant-available forms. The rhizosphere wakes up. And you can plant within minutes.

The system is designed from day one for edge AI intelligence, real-time diagnostics, treatment logging, predictive optimization based on soil data, weather, and crop needs, and future integration with digital-twin soil models.
